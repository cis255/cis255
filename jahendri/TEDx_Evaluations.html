TEDxSVSU

2015:

Main page:

        Formatted well in full screen and mobile. When screen is not full size some words and the picture hang off the border.

        I like that it displays the date, time and where event is located, this is the first thing I noticed.

        It explains what the event is and details about what will happen.

        Good color scheme, doesnt distract you from important details.

        Menu bar looks specific and you know where you will go.

Schedule:

        Organized and very informative.

        Shows you where to park and enter, which is useful for people unfamiliar with this event.

        Campus Map (PDF) button is useless and unhelpful.

Speakers:

        Speaker Application is first thing I saw.

        Very good summaries of speakers and accomplishments.

        Nice pictures, makes speakers look approachable.

        Explains how long speakers will have to talk.

        Pictures differ in sizes on mobile device, and the side picture is just thrown into the mix.

Exhibits:

        I like summary of the exhibits.

        Tells you exactly what will be there.

        Good videos on what will be there.

        Explains what most exhibits are, but some are missing and I dont know what they are.

        Videos play on screen and dont take you to youtube to watch.

News:

        Mobile is unorganized and several things didnt load.

        Hard to read newspaper clippings.

 

 

Contact:

        Good layout and references for more information

        Has links to Corsers TED Profile and official site.

        Email to get in contact

Volunteers:

        Link on how to volunteer

        Shows what you can volunteer for

        Writing still hangs off screen when not full screen

        List of volunteers

        Picture makes event look really enjoyable

More:

        No need for assignments tab, I dont think

        Background videos are kinda neat

        No real need for sample videos
