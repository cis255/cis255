Who spoke at TEDxSVSU

Speakers were identified by the Program Committee. To volunteer to speak at future events, submit the speaker application to: TEDxSVSU@gmail.com. Volunteer speakers are unpaid, but they are guaranteed a free ticket, a reserved seat in the audience when they are not volunteering.

Each talk will last about 10-15 minutes. Speakers will have 20 minutes maximum time, including time to be introduced and transition to/from backstage. See also: TEDx Speaker Guide.
