SkillsUSA

SkillsUSA is a career and technical student organization that encompasses skilled trades. The organization focuses on helping skilled trades students further their tecnical skills and leadership skills. My junior year of high school I joined our local SkillsUSA chapter as a machinist. Come my senior year I joined our local chapters governing body as the chapter historian. My time as historian allowed me to learn detail orientation and helped to further my leadership skills. Our chapters governing body also attended a SkillsUSA leadership conference which assisted us in furthering ourselves and our duties.
