About TED x SVSU

TEDxSVSU is an independently organized TED-style event, a full day of local, national and international speakers, music and artistic presenters, and also sponsors and exhibitors who showcase novel displays and activities. The target audience is SVSU and the surrounding community, especially students, parents, families, staff, faculty, and administration. The event attracts educated people throughout mid-Michigan and beyond. Anyone interested in TED-style talks is welcome to attend TEDxSVSU. Organizers and leaders of the event include SVSU students, faculty and staff. The project is supported by the SVSU Foundation Research Grant Program.
