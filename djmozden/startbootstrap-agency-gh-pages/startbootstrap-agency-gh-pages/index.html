Tickets

How do I buy tickets?

Call (989) 964-4295. Tickets are available now, but seats are very limited. Only 100 tickets will be sold in total. Whether you pay by check or by credit card, please call to confirm your seat.General admission ticket prices are $25 for attendees. Seating is free for volunteers, speakers, exhibitors and sponsors.

How do I pay by check?

Preferred form of payment is by check. Please make check payable to Saginaw Valley State University, referencing TEDxSVSU in the memo line. You can mail your $25 check to: Saginaw Valley State University Attn: Sponsored Programs 7400 Bay Road Zahnow 228 University Center, MI 48710. A receipt will be mailed to the address on the check.

How do I pay by credit card?

If you prefer to pay by credit card, that payment will need to be made by telephone. Please call Sponsored Programs, (989) 964-4295 and reference TEDxSVSU. Saginaw Valley State University will accept payment by VISA, Mastercard, and Discover, they will not accept American Express. You will be required to provide your name, credit card number, three digit security code, expiration date, address to mail receipt to (zip code required), and phone number in case of any issues.

About

  • What is TED?

    "TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world." Source: www.ted.com.

  • What is TEDx?

    "TEDx was created in the spirit of TED's mission, "ideas worth spreading." It supports independent organizers who want to create a TED-like event in their own community." Source: www.ted.com. See also: related article.

  • What is TEDxSVSU?

    TEDxSVSU is an independently organized TED-style event, a full day of local, national and international speakers, music and artistic presenters, and also sponsors and exhibitors who showcase novel displays and activities. (See: Official website.) The target audience is SVSU and the surrounding community, especially students, parents, families, staff, faculty, and administration. The event attracts educated people throughout mid-Michigan and beyond. Anyone interested in TED-style talks is welcome to attend TEDxSVSU. Organizers and leaders of the event include SVSU students, faculty and staff. The project is supported by the SVSU Foundation Research Grant Program.
