TEDxSVSU is back for our Third Annual TEDx Conference!

TEDxSVSU will be held at Saginaw Valley State University in Curtis Hall and the conference will be in Banquet Rooms A & B located on the second floor. It can be reached by stairs or elevator located by the SVSU Bookstore with signs and directions posted at entryways. Parking is available in front of Curtis Hall in lots J1 and J2.

​

As you've probably realized, you won't be receiving a physical ticket for TEDxSVSU. Instead, our registration volunteers will have your name (the name used to purchase tickets via PayPal) on our guest list. When you arrive on Friday, you'll check in at the registration table, give your name, and you'll be all set.

​

The doors will be open at 5:00pm and attendees are encouraged to explore the exhibits outside of the Banquet Rooms. Experience virtual reality, learn about wireless security, and become familiar with credit card sized computing!

Food and snacks will be provided before and during the conference. Please be courteous to the speakers and stay seated until their Talk has concluded before standing.

TEDxSVSU will begin promptly at 6:00pm with breaks in between speakers. For more information on speakers and their 'Ideas Worth Spreading' check out their information on our website under the Speakers tab!
