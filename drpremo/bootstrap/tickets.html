Tickets

How do I buy tickets?

Call (989) 964-4295. Tickets are available now, but seats are very limited. Only 100 tickets will be sold in total. Whether you pay by check or by credit card, please call to confirm your seat.

General admission ticket prices are $25 for attendees. Seating is free for volunteers, speakers, exhibitors and sponsors.

How do I pay by check?

Preferred form of payment is by check. Pease make check payable to Saginaw Valley State University, referencing TEDxSVSU in the memo line. You can mail your $25 check to:

Saginaw Valley State University
Attn: Sponsored Programs
7400 Bay Road
Zahnow 228
University Center, MI 48710

A receipt will be mailed to the address on the check.

How do I pay by credit card?

If you prefer to pay by credit card, that payment will need to be made by telephone. Please call Sponsored Programs, (989) 964-4295and reference TEDxSVSU. Saginaw Valley State University will accept payment by VISA, Mastercard, and Discover, they will not accept American Express. You will be required to provide your name, credit card number, three digit security code, expiration date, address to mail receipt to (zip code required), and phone number in case of any issues.
