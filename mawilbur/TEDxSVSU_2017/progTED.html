What exactly is TED?

"TED is a nonprofit devoted to spreading ideas, usually in the form of short, powerful talks (18 minutes or less). TED began in 1984 as a conference where Technology, Entertainment and Design converged, and today covers almost all topics — from science to business to global issues — in more than 100 languages. Meanwhile, independently run TEDx events help share ideas in communities around the world."
Source: www.ted.com

What exactly is TEDxSVSU?

TEDxSVSU is an annual TED-style event organized by individuals from Saginaw Valley State University involving an array of diverse speakers, including music and artistic presenters, as well as a number of sponsors and exhibitors that make their way to the event.

When and where is TEDxSVSU?

This event will be held at Saginaw Valley State University in Q4-2017.
The exact date and rooms that the event will take place at will be announced at a later date.
