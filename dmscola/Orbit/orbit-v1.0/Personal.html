Personal Profile

This page is about just a few of my personal interests and hobbies. Hopefully by reading this page you can get a better idea of who I am as a person.

Lacrosse

I have been playing lacrosse for 8 years. I am currently a member of the Saginaw Valley Men's Club Lacrosse Team. In high school, I played on the varsity team for 4 years at Heritage High School and played travel lacrosse as part of the Grand Blanc Lacrosse Coalition.

If you're interested in attending a SVSU lacrosse game or becoming a member of the team, please contact me at dmscola@svsu.edu

Basketball

Basketball has always been an interest of mine. I enjoy playing pickup games in my spare time and watching on TV. I love to talk about the NBA and my favorite team is the Detroit Pistons. I played guard at Heritage High School and my dream is to someday be able to dunk.

Fishing

I have always been an avid fishermen and definitely enjoy a morning on the boat. Walleye fishing with my dad on Lake St. Clair is by far my favorite type of fishing, but I enjoy many other types of recreational fishing. Some memorable trips I have taken over the years include sturgeon fishing in the Detroit River and fishing for walleye and bass on Mullet Lake.
